About the Doctor

Healing with Science,
Leading with Compassion

Dr. Rashedul Hassan Kanak is a distinguished Internal Medicine Specialist and medical educator based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. With over 18 years of clinical experience, he has built a reputation for diagnostic excellence and compassionate patient care across some of the country's most prestigious healthcare institutions.

Holding an MBBS from Dhaka Medical College and the prestigious FCPS fellowship in Internal Medicine from the Bangladesh College of Physicians and Surgeons, Dr. Kanak combines rigorous academic training with deep clinical intuition developed through years at the bedside.

Currently serving as Associate Professor of Medicine at Green Life Medical College and as a Medicine Specialist at Medinova Medical Services, Malibag Branch, he continues to shape the next generation of physicians while delivering world-class patient care. His clinical interests span internal medicine, clinical research, and healthcare management.
